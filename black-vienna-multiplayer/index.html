How to Play

The Goal

Three suspect cards were set aside at the start — those are "Black Vienna." The rest were dealt to players. Deduce which three were set aside by interrogating each other.

On Your Turn

Choose one of the three face-up investigation cards (each shows 3 suspect letters) and select another player to interrogate. They'll answer privately — placing 0–3 chips to show how many of those letters they hold. The questioned player then becomes the next asker.

Special Investigations

After everyone has been questioned at least once and 6+ investigations have occurred, you may also pair a used (face-down) card with your current card — giving the target two cards to answer at once.

Your Sheet

Use your investigation sheet to track results. Mark + (has card), − (doesn't have), or ○ (possible Black Vienna). Tap any cell to cycle.

Accusation

At any time tap Accuse and name the three criminals. Correct = you win. Wrong = you're eliminated from winning but keep playing. Score zero if wrong.

Scoring

Winner: 3 pts × chips remaining. Others who were correct at game end: 1 pt × chips remaining.

Card Distribution

3 players: 8 cards · 4 players: 6 cards · 5 players: dealer 4, others 5 · 6 players: 4 cards
